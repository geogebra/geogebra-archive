Groebner bases package.

This package contains classes for polynomial and solvable polynomial reduction, Groebner bases and ideal arithmetic as well as thread parallel and distributed versions of Buchbergers algorithm, e.g. ReductionSeq, GroebnerBaseAbstract, GroebnerBaseSeq, GroebnerBaseParallel and GroebnerBaseDistributed. Moreover there are Groebner bases in polynomial rings over principal ideal domains and Euclidean domains, so called D- and E-Groebner bases, see DGroebnerBaseSeq and EGroebnerBaseSeq. Groebner bases for polynomial rings over regular rings (direct products of fields or integral domains) are implemented in RGroebnerBaseSeq and RGroebnerBasePseudoSeq.
