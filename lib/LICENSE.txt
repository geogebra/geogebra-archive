This directory contains 3rd party libraries. Most of them are required
to run GeoGebra properly.


jlatexmath.jar, jlm_greek.jar, jlm_cyrillic.jar
-----------------------------------------------

These files are from Calixte Denizet's JLaTeXMath project. He provides a
minimal version for GeoGebra at
http://forge.scilab.org/index.php/p/jlatexmath/downloads/253/get/, but
GeoGebra also bundles a more recent version in its download repository.

License: GNU General Public License (GPL) 2 or above.
